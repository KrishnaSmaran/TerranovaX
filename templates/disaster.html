Disaster Prevention Tips

🔥 Fire Safety

Keep flammables away, install smoke detectors, maintain fire extinguishers.

🌊 Flood Safety

Move to higher ground, avoid water, and keep emergency kits ready.

🌪️ Storm Safety

Stay indoors, secure loose items, and follow alerts.

🌍 Earthquake Safety

Drop, cover, hold on, and keep emergency supplies ready.

Disaster Safety Stories

Cyclone 🌪️

🏃‍♂️ Move indoors to safe shelter
🪟 Close all windows and doors
📻 Listen to official alerts

Flood 🌊

⬆ Move to higher ground
🚫 Avoid walking/driving in floodwater
🎒 Keep emergency kit ready

Earthquake 🌍

🪑 Drop, Cover & Hold On
🚶 Evacuate if instructed
📱 Stay updated via alerts

Wildfire 🔥

🏃 Evacuate immediately
😷 Wear mask to avoid smoke
📻 Follow official updates

Heatwave ☀️

💧 Stay hydrated
🏠 Stay indoors during peak heat
🧊 Use cool compresses
